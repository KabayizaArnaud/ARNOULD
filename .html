Founded in 2018 in the heart of Kigali, Rwanda, Kigali Food was born from a passion for bold flavors and warm Rwandan hospitality. We blend international culinary techniques with locally-sourced ingredients from Rwandan farmers.

Every burger is grilled fresh to order, every side is prepared daily, and every delivery is packed with care. We believe exceptional food should be accessible to everyone in our beautiful city.
